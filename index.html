Automations & Systems Built

Legal Case Documentation Pipeline
Two-phase Python automation (prepare.py + merge.py) that downloads discounting agreements from Bubble.io CDN, extracts investor names via text parsing, organises Drive folders per investor, and assembles complete legal PDF packages ready for legal submission without any manual sorting. Covers 100–150 investors per case.
↓ 2–3 days of manual work per asset → minutes
