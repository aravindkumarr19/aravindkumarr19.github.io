A Day in Asset Operations — What Actually Happens Behind the Scenes

By Aravind · Asset Operations Intern · Chennai

Most people think fintech is about apps, UI, and transactions.

But behind every transaction, there’s a layer of operations that keeps everything running smoothly.

Working in asset operations, I’ve realized the real work is often invisible—but critical.

1. It starts with understanding the deal

Every day begins with understanding the structure, stakeholders, and fund flow behind a deal.

2. Financial model creation

Creating and validating financial models helps in structuring deals and understanding returns.

3. Agreement initiation

This includes:

  • Discounting Agreements
  • Indemnity Bonds
  • Guarantee Agreements
  • Term Sheets

4. VBA creation and KYC

Setting up Virtual Bank Accounts (VBAs) and completing KYC ensures proper tracking and compliance.

5. Vendor and asset setup

  • Vendor ID creation
  • Asset card creation

6. Fund flow coordination

Coordinating with finance teams to allocate and track funds correctly.

7. TDS and compliance

Ensuring TDS deductions and compliance requirements are handled properly.

8. Visibility and communication

Providing updates, statements, and clarity to stakeholders.

What I’ve learned

Asset operations sits at the intersection of finance, compliance, and coordination.
