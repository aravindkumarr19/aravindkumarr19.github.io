A Day in Asset Operations.
What Actually Happens Behind the Scenes.

By Aravind · Asset Operations Intern · Chennai

Most people associate fintech with seamless apps, instant transactions, and clean user interfaces.

But what users experience is only the surface.

Behind every transaction is a structured operational layer that ensures money moves correctly, risks are managed, compliance is maintained, and every stakeholder stays aligned.

Understanding the deal comes first

Every transaction begins with clarity—stakeholders, fund flow, timelines, and obligations.

Financial model creation

Financial models help structure deals, validate returns, and ensure assumptions align with reality.

Agreement initiation

  • Discounting Agreements
  • Indemnity Bonds
  • Guarantee Agreements
  • Term Sheets

VBA creation and KYC

Virtual Bank Accounts enable structured tracking of funds, while KYC ensures compliance.

Vendor & asset setup

  • Vendor ID creation
  • Asset card creation

Fund flow coordination

Ensuring correct allocation of funds and alignment with finance teams.

TDS and compliance

Managing deductions and ensuring regulatory requirements are met.

Visibility and communication

Providing stakeholders with clarity on balances, statements, and transactions.

What I’ve learned

Asset operations sits at the intersection of finance, compliance, and execution—ensuring everything works seamlessly in the background.

Closing perspective

Fintech may feel instant on the surface, but behind it is a coordinated system of processes and decisions that make it work.
